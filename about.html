Our Main Focus

At Tropika Harvest Berhad, we take pride in being a fruit farming enterprise operating entirely in Malaysia. Our company only sells local fruits of premium quality such as Durian, Manggis, Pisang and Rambutan. We ensure that every single fruit harvested are fresh, safe to eat and priced affordably for both local and international markets by managing our orchards directly and systematically.
